NBA Rules & Regulations

Teams must attempt a shot within 24 seconds of gaining possession.

The offense must advance the ball past the half-court line within 8 seconds.

Offensive players cannot remain in the paint for more than 3 seconds.

A player may not take more than two steps without dribbling.

Once a player stops dribbling, they cannot resume dribbling.

A player fouls out after reaching the limit of personal fouls.

Technical fouls are given for unsportsmanlike conduct.

Defenders cannot interfere with a shot on its downward trajectory.

Excessive or dangerous contact results in flagrant fouls and possible ejection.

When a shot hits the rim, the shot clock resets to 14 seconds under specific conditions.
